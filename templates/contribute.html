How to Contribute

Welcome to the Contribution Page! Here’s how you can contribute:

  • Submit Your Photos:

    If you have any photos you’d like to contribute, including handmade greeting cards or any meaningful images, please upload them. This can include personal pictures, event photos, or artistic creations.

    Use the provided upload form to submit your photos. Make sure to include a brief description and the name of the person in the photo.

  • Share Your Messages:

    If you don’t have photos to contribute, that’s perfectly fine! You can still contribute by sharing messages of appreciation or gratitude.

    Use the message submission form to send your messages. Each message will be displayed on a simple card with lobster font.

  • Automatic Card Generation:

    If you provide a message without an image, the app will automatically generate a card for it, ensuring your message is presented beautifully.

  • Download All Messages:

    You can download all the messages, including those with and without images, using the "Download" button on the gallery/index page.

Thank you for contributing and helping us make this gallery special!
